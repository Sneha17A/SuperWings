Be future ready with Montessori teacher training course

Happy learning!

Welcome to super Wings Montessori teachers training academy. Our super wings Academy provides Montessori kindergarten nursery and primary education for Aspiring teachers and entrepreneurs, parents in India and abroad.

Developed by Italian physician come educational list Dr Maria Montessori the Montessori method of education is child centred educational approach that focuses on the development of children physically socially cognitively and emotionally and if you are eager to get familiar with this learning method and work as a Montessori teacher then Super Wings Montessori teachers training academy will be the ideal choice for you. As a Montessori, you will teach children between the ages 2 to 8, You will guide and support the little Explorer to reach their personal Zenith with this teaching method. Our curriculum not only focuses on the teaching methodologies also on children's learning after the completion of the scores you will know how to teach children playfully and hard fully because teachers teach from the heart not from the book and also you will be guided for the internships and lead to placements.

Our levels in Montessori teacher training program as Montessori education is non-conventional educational method without being familiar with this it would be difficult for you to teach the children. We understand the role of Montessori teachers which plays in the life of children . so when you want to be a Montessori teacher you should ensure that you have the required skill and knowledge.We have divided our course into three types. Diploma Certificate Course, Advanced Diploma Certificate Course and ECC (early childhood and child care) education, We also teach Learning disability programme, Drawing, Phonetics, Abacus Handwriting and Calligraphy Duration for upcoming courses is 4 months and it is a basic course for Aspiring Montessori teachers during the scores.
